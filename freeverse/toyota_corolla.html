Toyota Corolla

My uncle has
six Toyota Corollas,
patient in the garage
and beside the curb
like a fleet
in the flesh.

He lives in a place
and in everything that
a place is.

She'll take the car,
my mother's daughter, and drive
to places that are only
places and nothing more

past Toyota Corollas like
a vision scrawled
onto the surface of a lake
or a word uttered from heaven
in the nick of time.

Toyotus Corollus
Corollas Corollos

She says there's no mistake about it...
through and through
and through freeways
through freeways

i saw it on the way
there to Auntie's house:
A vein, peeking through a break
in the skin. A fish whose mouth
breaks the surface of a lake
like dawn.
